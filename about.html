About Me

This is what I do.

I am a Computer Science Engineer transitioning into a Finance professional while pursuing my CFA. My career began at a boutique management consulting firm, where I gained valuable experience analyzing startups and creating roadmaps for companies in their embryonic stages.

In addition to my consulting work, I am an active investor in the Indian stock markets, with experience trading stocks, options, and cryptocurrency futures. I am also an aviation enthusiast, deeply passionate about aircraft.

In 2021, I served as the Vice President of Seva and Sahayog (Mulund) NGO, where I successfully organized various food, utensil, and clothing distribution drives.
